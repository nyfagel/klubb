Klubb

A system to manage your club. Open-Source and everything. Built using our favourite tools: CodeIgniter, Foundation and SQL.

What is it?

Klubb is an engine for a member database.

Who's it for?

Anybody who maintains a club or organization. It's built for the non-profit organization "Ung Cancer" so it's primarily designed to fit their needs.
